For those who aren't in the know:

Hi, I'm Mind (or at least, thats my alias.) I've been working on a Roblox game with another developer after becoming invested in their game after playing it. Arknights: Last Budget is a CO-OP PvE Campaign-Based First-Person Shooter/Action/Fighting game on Roblox, taking inspiration from Arknights, Decaying Winter, and (from my perspective) Ultrakill. You can select from a pleathora of different Operators (characters) with different classes and abilities to work with your fellow Operators and defeat various bad guys. For, you know. Lore reasons.

As for my specific involvement, I have been majorly involved in working on ALB since about the "Break That Ice" update (Around January). I work on various concepts, assembling character models, animating, creating enemies, and building stages. Essentially, I'm a jack of a decent amount of trades. I've enjoyed my time working on this project, from when I used to just play the game, to beginning to test it, to eventually becoming a dev for the game.
